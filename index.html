About Me

With over 16 years of experience in software engineering and backend systems, and recent hands-on experience in machine learning and data science, I am eager to contribute my expertise to innovative, data-driven research and product development efforts.


In my most recent role as Lead Engineer, I transitioned into the AI/ML space by leading the design and deployment of a real-time churn prediction model and a personalized recommendation system for a leading casual mobile game. These solutions, built using Python, scikit-learn, SHAP, and deployed with FastAPI and Vertex AI, improved player engagement and enabled product teams to make proactive design decisions. I also developed a Monte Carlo–based simulation model to support A/B testing and game feature optimization before launch.


This shift was fueled by a deep interest in applying data science to real-world problems. I’ve since pursued specialized training, built end-to-end ML systems, and integrated explainability into my workflow—combining rigorous engineering principles with modern AI methodologies. I am particularly excited by the opportunity to contribute to research and experimentation at scale, and to drive data-informed strategies within a collaborative, intellectually curious team.


I am confident that my blend of technical leadership, product intuition, and applied machine learning experience would allow me to add value to your data science and research efforts from day one. I welcome the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to your team’s goals.
